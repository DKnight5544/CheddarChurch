Welcome to CheddarChurch.com

"Come for church, bring some cheddar..."

CheddarChurch.com is here to bring a little humor, hustle, and maybe even a little grift into your faith journey. Whether you're chasing the cheddar or cutting the cheese with friends, there's a place for you here.

Our Mission Statement:

"Make America Grate Again!"

Our Sacred Practices (The Three Cheeses)

  • Breaking of the Cheese – In fellowship, we break cheese together (often with crackers and wine), celebrating both earthly bounty and heavenly joy.
  • Chasing the Cheese – We engage in holy hustle, believing that faithful effort leads to divine returns.
  • Cutting of the Cheese – We joyfully give a portion of our cheddar to support the mission of CheddarChurch.com and those in need.

The 10 Commandments of CheddarChurch.com

  • I am the Cheddar, thy Gouda. Thou shalt not have any gouda beside me.
  • Thou shalt not take the name of the Cheddar in vain, unless it’s really funny.
  • Remember the Cheese Day, and keep it tasty.
  • Honor thy cheese and thy crackers, that thy days may be long and delicious.
  • Thou shalt not let good cheese go moldy.
  • Thou shalt not cut the cheese... without opening a window.
  • Thou shalt not steal another's cheddar, but thou mayest borrow with permission.
  • Thou shalt not bear false witness. Thy grift must be subtle with a grain of truth.
  • Thou shalt not covet thy neighbor's Gouda, nor his Brie, nor his Havarti.
  • Thou shalt side-hustle faithfully for thy cheddar, and share it generously with thine upline.
